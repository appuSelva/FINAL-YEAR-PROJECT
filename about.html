About JustWage

JustWage is a platform built with a mission to empower and support local daily wage workers by connecting them directly with clients who need their services.

Many skilled laborers like electricians, plumbers, carpenters, painters, and general workers often go unnoticed due to lack of visibility. JustWage helps solve this by providing a digital presence for them—making it easier for people to find, trust, and hire them.

As a client, you can browse worker categories, view trusted profiles, contact them directly, and even leave reviews to support honest workers. We use Google Sheets as our backend to keep things simple, fast, and efficient.

This project was developed as a final-year academic project to make a real social impact. It's not just a website—it's a step toward better livelihoods.
